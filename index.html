Butter chicken

Butter chicken, traditionally known as Murgh makhani, is a type of curry which is made from chicken with a spiced tomato, butter sauce and Indian spices known as "Khada masalas". Its sauce is known for its rich texture. In addition sometimes the cream gives the curry sauce a silky smooth rich texture.

Dal Makhani

Dal Makhani is a restaurant style version with subtle smoky flavors and creaminess of the lentils. If you love authentic North Punjabi food then you are going to love this Dal Makhani even more.
